# Managing Bookings

## Setting availability {#setting-availability}

Availability tells owners which days and hours you are open to walk their dogs.

1. Go to **Profile → Walker profile → Availability**.
2. For each day of the week, tap the day and set your start and end times.
3. To mark a specific date as unavailable (holiday, personal day), tap **Exception dates** and add the date.
4. Tap **Save**. Availability is replaced in full — every save overwrites the previous schedule.

:::tip
Set a generous window. Owners pick specific slots within your availability, so you don't need to pre-block individual hours — just define the overall window when you're free.
:::

## Viewing incoming bookings

Tap the **My Bookings** option in the **Profile** tab (visible once you're an approved walker). Bookings are organised into four tabs:

| Tab | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Pending** | New booking requests waiting for your decision |
| **Upcoming** | Accepted bookings with future dates |
| **Completed** | Finished walks |
| **Cancelled** | Walks cancelled by you or the owner |

## Accepting a booking

On the **Pending** tab, each request shows:
- The scheduled date and time
- Duration and estimated pay
- Special instructions from the owner (if any)

Tap **Accept** to confirm. The booking moves to **Upcoming** and the owner receives a notification.

## Rejecting a booking

Tap **Reject** if you can't take the walk. A confirmation dialog appears. After rejecting, the booking disappears from your list and the owner is notified. You cannot undo a rejection.

## Cancelling an accepted booking

On the **Upcoming** tab, tap **Cancel** on the booking. A confirmation dialog asks you to confirm. Use this only when necessary — repeated cancellations may affect your rating.

## The Start Walk button

On the **Upcoming** tab, the **Start Walk** button appears on a booking card starting **15 minutes before** the scheduled slot. It remains available until the slot end time.
