# Getting Started as a Walker

## 1. Create a regular account first

Walkers use the same login system as dog owners. If you don't have an account yet, sign up normally from the login screen.

## 2. Apply to become a walker

1. Go to the **Profile** tab.
2. Tap **Become a Walker**.
3. Fill in the application form:
   - **Identity document** — upload a photo of your ID
   - **Work location** — the area where you offer walks
   - **Experience** — years of experience with dogs
   - **Description** — a short bio that owners will see on your public profile
4. Tap **Submit application**.

Your application will be reviewed. You'll receive a notification when it is approved or rejected.

## 3. Set up your walker profile

Once approved, the **Walker** section appears in your Profile tab. Fill in:

- **Bio** — a detailed description of your experience and approach
- **Hourly rate** — price per hour in your local currency
- **Service radius** — how far from your work location you are willing to travel (km)
- **Years of experience**
- **Accepting bookings** — toggle this on when you are available to take new bookings, off when you are not

## 4. Set your availability

Owners can only book slots within your declared availability. Without availability configured, no slots appear on your public profile and no bookings can be made.

[Learn how to set your availability →](/user-guide/walker/managing-bookings#setting-availability)

## 5. Your profile is now live

Owners browsing the Services tab will see your profile card with your name, photo, rating, hourly rate, and availability status. Make sure your **Accepting bookings** toggle is on.
