# Getting Started as a Dog Owner

## 1. Create an account

Open Balto and tap **Sign Up**. Fill in your first and last name, email address, and a secure password. Tap **Create account**.

If you already have an account, tap **Log in** and enter your credentials. Check **Remember me** if you want the app to keep you signed in after closing it.

## 2. Complete your profile

After logging in, tap the **Profile** tab (paw icon). Tap **Edit profile** to:

- Upload a profile photo (optional but recommended so walkers can identify you)
- Verify your name and contact details

## 3. Add your pets

Before you can book a walk you must add at least one pet.

1. Go to **Profile → Pets** (or tap the pet card on the Home screen).
2. Tap the **+** button.
3. Enter your pet's name, species, breed, date of birth, weight, and an optional photo.
4. Tap **Save**.

You can add multiple pets and edit or remove them at any time from the same screen.

## 4. Explore the Home screen

The Home screen shows a personalised dashboard:

- **Live walk card** — appears automatically when a walk is in progress. Tap it to open the live map.
- **Upcoming walk card** — your next scheduled walk with the date and time.
- **Daily tip** — a rotating dog-care tip that changes each day.
- **Quick care grid** — shortcuts to Walkers, Vets, Stores, and your Pets.
