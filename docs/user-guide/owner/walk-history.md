# Walk History & Summaries

After a walk ends you can review a summary with stats and the full GPS route.

## Viewing today's completed walks

Completed walks from today appear in the **Walks** tab under the **"Completed Today"** section. They are listed from the most recent walk to the oldest.

Tap any walk card to open the **Walk Summary** screen.

## Walk Summary screen

The Walk Summary screen shows:

- **Full route map** — the polyline traces every GPS point recorded during the walk. Green marker = start, red marker = end.
- **Distance** — total kilometres walked, calculated from GPS data.
- **Duration** — how long the walk lasted.

The map auto-fits the camera to show the entire route when it loads. Tap the **Done** button to return to the previous screen.

:::info No route data?
If the walker's phone lost signal for the entire walk or permissions were denied, the map will show "No route data recorded" instead of the polyline, but the distance and duration summary is still shown.
:::

## Viewing older walks

All completed walks (not just today's) are visible in the **Walks** tab under the **History** section, sorted newest first. Tap any completed walk in History to open the same Walk Summary screen.

Cancelled and rejected bookings also appear in History but do not have a route to view.

## Walks tab sections explained

| Section | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **In Progress** | A walk currently happening |
| **Pending** | Booking requests waiting for walker response |
| **Upcoming** | Accepted bookings with a future date |
| **Completed Today** | Walks that finished today, newest first |
| **History** | All older completed, cancelled, and rejected bookings |
