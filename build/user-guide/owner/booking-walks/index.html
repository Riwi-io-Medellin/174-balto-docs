Booking a Walk

Finding a walker​

  1. Tap the Services tab (people icon) in the bottom navigation.
  2. The Walkers section lists available walkers near you. You can see their name, rating, price per hour, and whether they are currently accepting bookings.
  3. Tap a walker's card to see their full profile: biography, experience, reviews, service area, and gallery.
tip

Look for the green "Accepting bookings" badge. Walkers without it are temporarily unavailable.

Starting the booking​

On a walker's profile page, tap Book Now. This opens the booking wizard.

Step 1 — Select your pet​

The wizard shows all the pets on your account. Tap the pet you want to book the walk for. If you have no pets yet, you'll see a prompt to add one first.

Step 2 — Select duration​

Choose how long you want the walk to last:

  • 30 minutes
  • 45 minutes
  • 60 minutes
  • 90 minutes

The price estimate updates automatically based on the walker's hourly rate and your chosen duration.

Step 3 — Select date​

Tap the calendar icon to pick a date. The wizard only shows dates when the walker has availability configured.

Step 4 — Select a time slot​

After choosing a date, the available time slots for that day appear as a scrollable list. Tap your preferred slot.

Slot conflicts

If someone else books the same slot between when you loaded the list and when you confirm, the app will alert you with a conflict notice and refresh the list so you can pick another slot.

Step 5 — Add instructions (optional)​

Use the notes field to add anything the walker should know: your dog's temperament, the meeting point, gate code, allergies, or special care instructions.

Confirming the booking​

Tap Confirm booking. A confirmation screen appears with the booking details. The booking now appears in the Pending section of your Walks tab.

What happens next?​

StatusMeaning
PendingThe walker has received your request and hasn't responded yet
UpcomingThe walker accepted — the walk is confirmed
RejectedThe walker declined — you can try booking another walker

You'll receive a notification when the walker accepts or rejects your request.

Cancelling a booking​

You can cancel a Pending or Upcoming booking:

  1. Go to the Walks tab.
  2. Find the booking and tap it.
  3. Tap Cancel Booking and confirm.

Cancelled bookings move to the History section with "Cancelled" status.
